What is E-Waste?

Electronic waste (E-Waste) includes discarded devices like computers, phones, televisions, and batteries. Improper disposal releases harmful chemicals into the environment.

EcoConnect helps people responsibly dispose of electronic waste by connecting households with certified recycling workers.
